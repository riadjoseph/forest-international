Privacy Policy

Please read our privacy policy to learn how data is used on our website

We use cookies to provide the webiste service,  social media updates, and other usability features to analyse our traffic and present the content on the website.

We also share information about your use of our site with social media, advertising and analytics services who may combine it with other information that you’ve provided to them or that they’ve collected from your use of their services. These serevices are Facebook, Google Analytics, Google AdWords, Google Tag Manager, Inspectlet and their partners, and the core functions on our servers that make the website work for example the contact form. 

By filling the contact form, or using the chat service, you are requesting information from us and expect us and giving us the right to use your contacts given to reply to your message. You can ask us to delete/adjust your information anytime and we will do so. We will never emails, phone numbers, addresses, names with any third party. Your information on Facebook are not under our control but between you and Facebook.

How do we protect your information?
We do not use PCI standards, SSL certificates, because we do not ask for payments on the website. We only provide articles and information about our school. We never ask for credit card numbers. We use regular Malware Scanning. We store your information on the server protected by third parties and only admins have access to that server. 

Did you know? 
You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies. You do this through your browser settings. Since every browser is a little different, look at your browser's Help Menu to learn the correct way to modify your cookies. 

If you turn cookies off, Some of the features that make your site experience more efficient may not function properly but you will still be able to read the information and see our phone number.

Opting out:
Users can set preferences for how Google advertises to you using the Google Ad Settings page. Alternatively, you can opt out by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative Opt Out page or by using the Google Analytics Opt Out Browser add on.

How does our site handle Do Not Track signals?
We honor Do Not Track signals and Do Not Track, plant cookies, or use advertising when a Do Not Track (DNT) browser mechanism is in place.

Does our site allow third-party behavioral tracking?
It's also important to note that we allow third-party behavioral tracking, anonymously so we enhance our website and the users' experience.

Children
We do not specifically market to children or any person under the age of 21 years old. Our website is safe for all ages and we recommend you sit with your child to view our website. Children should have an adult's guidance and supervision when handling online information.
